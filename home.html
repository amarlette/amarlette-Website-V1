About Me

I work with both hardware and software as an assistant manager at the Georgia Institute of Technology College of Computing where I help introduce young students to computer science through hands on activities I designed. I have created original curriculum using Arduinos to create remote controlled penguins, programmable light-up sunglasses, and hat. My passion computer science illustrates itself in my work with Call of the Code, an organization I founded to address the lack of computer science education in my community. During the summer of 2016 I interned with Deloitte US LP with the Information Technology, there I have sharpened my skills at communicating with three different teams in the Database Services department, creating Virtual Machines, and creating new servers. On top of that I and another intern created dashboards for three teams that collected their data and kept it up to date via Tableau and SharePoint.
